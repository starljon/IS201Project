Frequently Asked Questions

For the Curious Folk

Can I go through a car wash with a vinyl wrap?
Yes. You can take your wrapped vehicle through an automated brushless car wash.

How long does a vinyl wrap last for?
Vinyl wraps typically last for 5-7 years before beginning to peel, fade, or crack.

How can I prolong my vinyl's lifetime?
To prolong your vinyl's lifetime, wash your car regularly and use covered parking when possible.

Should I wash my car before getting it wrapped?
Yes. In order for the vinyl to adhere correctly, avoid the formation of bubbles, and keep the vehicle scratch-free, it needs to be cleaned of dust, mud, oil, and anything else that may be on the surface of the vehicle.

I want to sell my car. What do I do with the vinyl?
You can choose to retain the wrap or remove it to return the car to its original state. Car wraps are completely removable and don’t damage the original paint, so your car’s resale value is retained.

How long does a vinyl wrap last for?
Most car wraps last approximately five to seven years. After five to seven years most vinyl wraps will begin to crack, fade, and peel off the vehicle.

Can I get a wrap if my car has dents?
Yes, you can wrap a car with dents, but again, the vinyl wrap will not hide the dents though it does provide a layer of protection against minor dings and scratches. For the best car wrap results, it would be best to fix the dents first and then apply the vinyl vehicle wrap.
